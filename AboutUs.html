Why FairPy: Compare to Other Libraries

FairPy aims to provide a comprehensive and unified library for machine learning fairness. It stands out from other libraries by offering a wide range of fairness algorithms, data structures, and learning tasks to cater to the needs of researchers and practitioners in the field.
